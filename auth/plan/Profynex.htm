Frequently Asked Questions

How do payments work?

Payments are processed securely using major cards and supported payment providers. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel depending on your plan terms.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Your plan remains active until the billing period ends.

Is there a free trial?

Starter is free forever. Premium and Enterprise can be trialed based on onboarding offers or sales arrangements.

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

Yes. Plan changes are available from your dashboard. Proration rules can be configured based on your billing policy.

Do you offer refunds?

Refund policy is configurable. For premium services, you can define a support and refund window in your billing settings.
